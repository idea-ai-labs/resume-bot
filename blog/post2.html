Top Resume Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

Learn the common pitfalls and tips to make your resume stand out to recruiters.

Resumes are your first impression with employers, so avoiding common mistakes is critical. Many job seekers make errors in formatting, clarity, and storytelling that can prevent their resume from reaching a human reader.

Common Mistakes

  • Poor formatting that’s hard to read.
  • Lack of clear action verbs or quantifiable results.
  • Generic descriptions that don’t highlight unique skills.
  • Overloading with irrelevant experiences.

How to Avoid Them

Use clean layouts, tailor your resume to the job, focus on measurable achievements, and maintain a consistent style. Simple AI tools can help identify inconsistencies and highlight key areas for improvement.
