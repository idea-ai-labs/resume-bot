Resume Tips for Career Changers

Showcase transferable skills, frame your experience, and impress recruiters when switching careers.

Changing careers can be exciting but challenging when updating your resume. The key is highlighting transferable skills and relevant accomplishments while keeping your resume concise and clear.

Highlight Transferable Skills

Identify skills from previous roles that apply to the new field. Emphasize leadership, communication, or technical skills relevant to your target job.

Reframe Your Experience

Instead of listing every previous role, focus on experiences that demonstrate the competencies your new career requires. Tailor descriptions to align with the job posting.

Keep It Professional

One-page, clean formatting helps recruiters quickly understand your potential. Avoid clutter and emphasize results over responsibilities.
